What is SiteMorph?

SiteMorph is an AI-powered tool that analyzes existing websites and automatically generates a more modern, clearer, and conversion-ready version in minutes without rebuilding the site from scratch.

How does it work?

You enter a website URL, SiteMorph analyzes structure, content, and UX, then selects layouts and rewrites content to generate an optimized preview you can review and publish.

How much does it cost?

You can start for free with a preview. Paid plans are based on how many websites you want to optimize, with simple per-site pricing and no long-term commitment.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes. You can generate a free preview without a credit card to see how SiteMorph improves your website before upgrading.

Do I need technical skills to use it?

No. SiteMorph is designed for business owners and teams. Everything runs automatically. No coding or design experience required.
